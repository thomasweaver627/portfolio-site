Bio

Thomas Weaver is a growing developer in the Greensboro Triad area of North Carolina. He has 3 years of experience in building automation tools with Microsoft Access, Excel, and Visual Basic for Applications (VBA) for the second largest credit union in the United States. While his current position is limited to these technologies, he is always trying to expand his programming knowledge and skills, such as learning JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. With over 7 years of support experience, he is passionate about customer success, data-driven decisions, and efficient and iterative design
